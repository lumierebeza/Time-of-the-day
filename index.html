Time of Day

A JavaScript Project by BEZAWIT TESERA

This webpage dynamically changes its background color, image, and greeting
message based on the current time of day (morning, afternoon, evening, night)
using JavaScript. It also displays the current day of the week and real-time clock,
updating every second. Additionally, the footer includes a dynamically updated copyright year.
The content and appearance adjust automatically, providing users with a visually and contextually
relevant experience depending on the time they visit the page.
